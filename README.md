# 🔍 VR Investigation Simulation

A virtual reality experience focused on environmental inspection and incident analysis within an industrial setting.

---

## 🎮 Controls

### 🕹 Movement
- **Left Controller Joystick**
  - Move forward, backward, and strafe.

### 🔄 Rotation
- **Right Controller Joystick**
  - Rotate (snap or smooth turn based on implementation).

---

## 🧭 Investigation Flow

Follow the sequence below to complete the investigation:

1. **Inspect Fire Extinguisher**
   - Check condition, accessibility, and validity.

2. **Inspect Fire Safety Signage**
   - Look for visibility, clarity, and maintenance status.

3. **Inspect Impacted Forklift Battery**
   - Observe placement, damage, and safety compliance.

4. **Inspect Fallen Steel Shelf**
   - Analyze cause of collapse and surrounding hazards.

---

## ✅ Objective

- Visit all investigation points in sequence.
- Observe carefully and identify potential safety issues.
- Complete the full inspection to finish the simulation.

---

## ⚠️ Notes

- Ensure you are facing objects directly to interact.
- Some interactions may require proximity.
- Follow the order strictly for proper flow completion.

---

## 🛠️ Setup (Optional)

- VR headset with controller support required.
- Recommended: Room-scale setup for better immersion.
